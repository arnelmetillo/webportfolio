After working as SHS teacher, I decided to pursue my acquired passion about coding and programming. Work Experience

Wayback 2017, I was appointed to try my luck at Taguig National High School as Special Science Teacher 1 based on R.A. 10612 or the "Fast-Tracked S&T Scholarship Act of 2013" since I was once a DOST Scholar. In return of the scholarship program, I rendered my service for almost 5 years as a teacher. During my endeavor as an educator, I was first designated as Science Club adviser from school year 2018 up until school year 2021. It was quite tough obligation for I have no idea at all and nowhere to start. With the helping hand of my co-teachers in the department, I was able to do and perform well as a Club Adviser. Then, during the length of my service, I was also appointed as the SHS Enrollment Focal Person. This task was very challenging for I handled 800 plus enrollees.

Teachers' duties and tasks are very overwhelming unlike other professions.
